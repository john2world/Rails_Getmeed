INTRODUCTION

Our goal is to help students build detailed, engaging, online resumes — whether they are experienced postgraduate students or freshmen navigating their first semester. Students on Meed create a profile that includes their past experience, education and courses, a portfolio of projects they’ve completed, and thoughts on their favorite career-related topics. Once they have completed their profile, students enter a community of alumni, recruiters, industry leaders, and their peers who are dedicated to launching their careers.

6 REASONS MEED IS THE BEST

Rich Profiles

Student profiles on Meed have more information than other platforms and resumes. These rich and always up-to-date profile allow you to gain a more comprehensive understanding of your job applicants.

Quality

Your job postings on Meed can be targeted, eliminating the process of scouting through applications that don't match the qualifications. The rich and structured profile information enables you to further refine your applicants by many different criteria.

Diversity

Students on Meed come from different universities and locations. Regardless of where you or your positions are located, you have access to a diverse set of candidates.

Branding

Meed gives companies the unique opportunity to establish their brand among students. Your presence helps them learn more about your company while helping you grow your company’s audience.

Engage

Hiring students is only one facet of Meed. As a part of our network, you can connect with, follow, and engage with top student talent.

Cost

Hiring on Meed costs a fraction of what other traditional college recruiters charge. Designed to scale your hiring needs while keeping the cost love, we have saved over 15% of recruiting budgets for our clients.
